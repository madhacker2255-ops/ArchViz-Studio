Project Overview

Modern Haven is a private residence located in Bengaluru, designed for a young family that values minimalism, natural light, and a strong connection with nature.

The design emphasizes clean lines, open spaces, and a neutral material palette that creates a calm and timeless atmosphere.

Large openings and thoughtfully planned courtyards ensure abundant daylight and natural ventilation throughout the home.

Natural Light Maximized daylight through large openings.
Sustainable Eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient design.
Open Spaces Seamless flow between indoor and outdoor areas.
Warm Materials Natural materials bring warmth and character.
